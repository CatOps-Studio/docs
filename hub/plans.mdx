## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Personal | Pro |
|---------|----------|-----|
| **Price** | $5/month | $12/month |
| **Data Retention** | 24 hours | 30 days |
| **Docker Containers** | Up to 10 | Unlimited |
| **Servers** | 1 | 3 |

## Personal - $5/month

Best for personal projects and development servers.

- Real-time monitoring dashboard
- Docker container management (up to 10)
- Service detection
- Network monitoring
- 24-hour metrics history
- 1 server

## Pro - $12/month

Best for production servers and teams.

Everything in Personal, plus:
- 30-day metrics retention
- Unlimited containers
- Up to 3 servers

## Upgrading

1. Purchase Pro at [catops.app/hub](https://catops.app/hub)
2. Find your new license key at [catops.app/billing](https://catops.app/billing) in the Hub tab
3. Deactivate current license: `sudo -u catops /opt/catops/catops-hub deactivate`
4. Enter your new Pro license key

Your data is preserved during the upgrade.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch between plans?">
    Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Your data is preserved (within the new plan's retention limit).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if my license expires?">
    Hub redirects to the activation page. Your data is preserved. Renew to continue.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use one license on multiple servers?">
    Personal: 1 server. Pro: up to 3 servers.
  </Accordion>

  </Accordion>
