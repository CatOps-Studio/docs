CatOps Hub offers two plans to fit your needs.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Personal | Pro |
|---------|----------|-----|
| **Price** | $5/month | $12/month |
| **Data Retention** | 24 hours | 30 days |
| **Containers** | Up to 10 | Unlimited |
| **Server Activations** | 1 | 3 |
| **Alerts** | Basic (in-app) | Email alerts |
| **Support** | Community | Priority |

## Personal Plan

<Card title="$5/month" icon="user">
  Best for personal projects, development servers, and small VPS instances.
</Card>

**Includes:**
- Real-time monitoring dashboard
- Docker container management (up to 10 containers)
- Service detection
- Network monitoring
- 24-hour metrics history
- Basic in-app alerts
- 1 server activation

## Pro Plan

<Card title="$12/month" icon="building">
  Best for production servers, agencies, and teams that need longer history.
</Card>

**Everything in Personal, plus:**
- 30-day metrics retention
- Unlimited containers
- Email alerts (coming soon)
- Up to 3 server activations
- Priority support

## Upgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Purchase Pro">
    Visit [catops.app/hub](https://catops.app/hub) and purchase Pro plan
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deactivate Current">
    On your server:
    ```bash
    catops-hub deactivate
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate New">
    Enter your new Pro license key in Settings or `/activate`
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your data is preserved during the upgrade.

## Downgrading

If you downgrade from Pro to Personal:

- Data retention changes to 24 hours
- Metrics older than 24 hours will be deleted at the next hourly cleanup
- Container limit of 10 will apply

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch between plans?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Your metrics data is preserved (within the new plan's retention limit).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if my license expires?">
    Hub will continue running but redirect to the activation page. Your data is preserved. Renew or reactivate to continue using Hub.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use one license on multiple servers?">
    - **Personal**: 1 server only
    - **Pro**: Up to 3 servers

    Need more? Contact support for volume pricing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Not currently. The Personal plan at $5/month is designed to be accessible for everyone.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
    We accept credit/debit cards through Polar. More payment methods coming soon.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Contact support within 7 days of purchase for a full refund if Hub doesn't meet your needs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
